About Us




Welcome to Al-Baz – Power in Motion

Based in the heart of Dubai, Al-Baz, meaning The Hawk, symbolizes speed, precision, and vision – values that drive our dual services in car rentals and freight logistics across the UAE.


Whether you're a traveler seeking a smooth ride across the city or a business moving goods across borders, Al-Baz is your trusted partner for reliability and performance. With a commitment to excellence, efficiency, and customer satisfaction, we aim to redefine transport services in the region.


We offer a wide range of vehicles – from economy cars to premium models – tailored for individuals, tourists, and corporate clients. Our flexible plans, subscription options, and 24/7 customer support ensure a seamless rental experience.


Freight division provides reliable, fast, and secure cargo transportation across the UAE and GCC. From bulk deliveries to business logistics, we bring professionalism and punctuality to every shipment.
