CAR CARE & TRAVEL GUIDE

Smooth Journeys & Well-Maintained Vehicles


Car Care Tips in the UAE

1.Regular AC Maintenance:

Due to UAE’s extreme heat, ensure your car’s air conditioning is serviced frequently for optimal cooling and comfort.

2.Check Tire Pressure Often:

Hot road surfaces can increase tire pressure. Check tires weekly to prevent blowouts and maintain fuel efficiency.

3.Protect Your Car from the Sun:

Use windshield sunshades and park in shaded areas to prevent interior damage and overheating.

4.Battery Checks are Essential:

Car batteries can drain quickly in hot weather. Have your battery tested regularly to avoid breakdowns.

5.Fluid Levels & Engine Cooling:

Monitor engine oil, coolant, and brake fluid levels frequently to keep the car performing safely.

6.Frequent Car Washes:

Sand and dust are common in the UAE. Regular car washes help protect the paint and visibility.


Travel Guide for Driving in the UAE

1.Drive on the Right Side:

The UAE follows right-hand driving. Ensure you're familiar with local traffic signs and road markings.

2.Speed Limits & Fines:

Speed limits vary (usually 60–120 km/h). Radar enforcement is common, and fines can be issued automatically.

3.Salik Tolls in Dubai:

Many Dubai roads have Salik (toll) gates. Your rental vehicle will be equipped with a tag that deducts tolls electronically.

4.Desert Driving Precautions:

If venturing off-road or into the desert, use a 4x4, reduce tire pressure, and always travel with proper supplies and communication gear.

5.Respect Local Laws & Customs:

Avoid aggressive driving, always wear your seatbelt, and never use your phone while driving.

6.Navigation & Apps:

Use GPS apps like Google Maps or Waze. Also consider RTA Dubai and Abu Dhabi Police apps for local traffic updates.
